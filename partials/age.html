Orthogonal latin squares

The construction of 4x4 set was published by Jacques Ozanam in 1725 as a puzzle involving playing cards.
The problem was to take all aces, kings, queens and jacks from a standard deck of cards, and arrange them in a 4x4 grid such that each row and each column contained all four suits as well as one of each face value.
